About

Codex is a platform for all tech geeks. The club will act as a helping hand to all people who want to learn a new technology. This club is all about learning together and helping each other so that all people of club grow with each other. Our vision is that every student has an opppourtunity to explore and learn beyond the curriculum.

Vision

Phase 1: Coding Game

In this phase, members are taught beginner programming concepts through gaming. It's not only informative, but it's also fun!

Phase 2

Domain Time!

In this section workshops will be conducted over unique domains. In those workshops Projects will be built with the participants. By the end of phase-2 our target is to deliver at least 3 full furnished projects in unique and cross domains. This section is specially made in a way that we upskill the participants so that they will be ready to compete in International Hackathons.
