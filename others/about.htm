About

Codex is a platform for all technology enthusiastic minds.
The club will act as a helping hand to all people who want to learn a new technology.
This club is all about learning together and helping each other so that all people of club grow with each other.
Our vision is that every student has an opppourtunity to explore and learn beyond the curriculum.


VISION


PHASE 1

Coding game

It is a process of teaching a Programming language to beginners via gamming. Learning Programming concepts via gaming.



PHASE 2

Domain Time

In this section workshops will be conducted over unique domains. In those workshops Projects will be built with the participants. By the end of phase-2 our target is to deliver at least 3 full furnished projects in unique and cross domains. This section is specially made in a way that we upskill the participants so that they will be ready to compete in International Hackathons.

Competitive Coding

Programming is fun, programming is a mental sport and when this sport is held over the internet involving sport programmer as a contestant then it is called Competitive Programming. We are Going to host weekly contests by the mid of phase 2.Contests which are beginner friendly but still challenging.Its format will be a mix of Code-Chef’s Long Challenge & Code-Forces Div-3 rounds. More details will be released soon!



PHASE 3

Hackathons

As soon as we complete Phase 2, Hackathons will be hosted inside GITAM. It will be between GITAMites. Our target is to have at least one Intra Hackathon once in two months.

Dogfooding

Eating your own dog food or dogfooding is the practice of an organization using its own product. We will start open source projects and interested people can contribute.
